Welcome to InstaPDF Tools ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website instapdftools.com, which provides free online PDF and image processing tools.

✅ Your files are processed entirely in your browser and are NEVER uploaded to our servers.

📂 File Processing & Your Data

InstaPDF Tools processes all files directly in your browser using JavaScript. This means:

  • Your files never leave your device

  • No file data is sent to our servers
  • Your documents remain 100% private and secure
  • Files are automatically cleared when you close the browser tab

We do NOT collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.
